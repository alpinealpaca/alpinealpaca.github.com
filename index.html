About Me

Me and my merry band of alpacas have been going around making alpaca related art for years now. Alpacas make an excellent crew as they not only know how to pose for the camera, but want to. Or at least most of them want to when you're holding food. Although they haven't told me this, I assume that the reason that they all got so into joining my art is actually because one day Bob the white alpaca once saw himself in the clouds. This must have sparked his desire to be in the spotlight. So once I told him that more people would see him in "The Cloud" online than the actual clouds, I'm pretty sure he was all on board and rallied his mates to get behind this noble cause.
